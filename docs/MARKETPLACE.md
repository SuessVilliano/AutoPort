# AutoPort — GHL Marketplace Listing

## App Name
**AutoPort — Phone Number Porting**

## Tagline
Port your phone numbers to GoHighLevel in minutes, not weeks.

## Short Description
AutoPort automates phone number porting for GHL agency owners and sub-account users. Connect your phone account, check number eligibility, upload documents, and submit port requests — all through a guided, step-by-step interface with real-time status tracking and email notifications.

## Full Description

### The Problem
Porting phone numbers is confusing, slow, and error-prone. Agency owners waste hours on back-and-forth with carriers, miss critical steps, and get hit with rejections that delay everything by weeks.

### The Solution
AutoPort streamlines the entire porting process into a simple 6-step wizard:

**Step 1: Connect Your Account**
Enter your Account SID and Auth Token. Works with LC Phone, GHL native phone, or any Twilio-powered system. Your credentials are used securely per-request and never stored.

**Step 2: Check Eligibility**
Enter phone numbers manually or upload a CSV for bulk checking. AutoPort instantly verifies each number's portability through the carrier API.

**Step 3: Enter Account Details**
Provide the account holder information exactly as it appears on your carrier records. AutoPort guides you on what carriers look for to prevent rejections.

**Step 4: Upload Documents**
Upload your phone bill (PDF). Choose your LOA mode — single document for all numbers, per-carrier, or per-number for complex situations.

**Step 5: Review & Submit**
Review everything before submitting. Generate a preview LOA if needed. Submit with one click.

**Step 6: Track Progress**
Get email updates at every stage — submission confirmation, LOA ready to sign, submitted to carrier, FOC date confirmed, and port complete. Track all requests in the My Requests dashboard.

### Built-In Porting Assistant
Not sure about something? The AI-powered porting assistant answers your questions instantly — timelines, rejection fixes, credential locations, toll-free requirements, and more.

### Key Features
- Real-time number portability checking
- Bulk CSV import for multi-number ports
- Automated LOA (Letter of Authorization) generation
- 6 automated email notifications per port request
- My Requests dashboard with live status tracking
- AI porting assistant for instant help
- Port rejection fix & resubmit flow
- Works with LC Phone, GHL native, and direct Twilio accounts

### Pricing
Free to use. Standard phone system rates apply for ported numbers.

## Installation Instructions for Users

### Finding Your Credentials

**If you use LC Phone / GHL Native Phone:**
1. Log in to your GHL account
2. Go to **Settings** → **Phone Integration**
3. Copy your **Account SID** (starts with AC) and **Auth Token**

**If you use Twilio directly:**
1. Log in to **console.twilio.com**
2. Your Account SID and Auth Token are on the main dashboard
3. Use a subaccount SID if porting to a specific subaccount

### Porting Your First Number
1. Go to https://auto-port.vercel.app
2. Paste your Account SID and Auth Token
3. Enter the email where you want status updates
4. Click **Connect & Verify Account**
5. Enter the phone number you want to port
6. Click **Check Eligibility**
7. Fill in your account holder info (must match your current carrier exactly)
8. Upload your phone bill as a PDF
9. Agree to the terms and review your request
10. Click **Submit Port Request**
11. Check your email to sign the Letter of Authorization
12. Wait 5-15 business days for the port to complete

### Tips to Avoid Rejections
- Name must match your carrier records **exactly** (including middle initials, LLC, Inc)
- Address must match your carrier's service address (not mailing address)
- Keep your number **active** with your current carrier until the port is done
- If rejected, use the fix link in your email — most rejections are fixable in minutes

### After Porting
- SMS may be unavailable for up to 3 business days (normal)
- Register for A2P 10DLC to send business text messages
- Set up your GHL workflows and phone trees for the ported number
- You can now cancel service with your old carrier
